The Akan Name Generator!

Hello there!This is a web application that takes your birthday details and based on your gender it generates your Akan name that corresponds with your birthday.In Ghana children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day of the week they were born.

To find out your Akan name,kindly fill the form below and click generate name button to display your Akan name.Lets explore Ghanian names
